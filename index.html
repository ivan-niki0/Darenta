but also collectibles!

Have you ever thought there is an IT platform exists, that allows to Decentrally Autonomously RENT an Auto? Have you ever wondered you can earn some money with your own car?

We are introducing Darenta carsharing. It transforms into a decentralized autonomous car rental service, that is not managed by people nor executives. Regulation on this decentralized IT platform will occur based on the feedback and recommendations by users, who will be registered via smart contracts. If for any reason a car owner constantly ignore requests from renter, the car owner is banned. Instead, if a car owner regularly rents out his car to renters and regularly receives positive feedback, his profile gets higher ranking in search results both in mobile applications and the website.

Users with good reputation will receive bonuses in the form of the promotion through our social networks groups. DARENTA’s users will be able in the future to earn tokens by posting their feedback. Token holders will allow users to manage the platform by regulating relationships. All of these without the direct participation by founders of the startup.

FAQ
Question:
How to rent out own car?
Answer:

Option 1. Register or log in to your account on the site, specify the parameters of your vehicle and upload a photo.

Option 2. You need to install the application (iOS or Android), register in the application or enter your personal account, specify the parameters of your car and upload the photo.

Question:
How to rent a car?
Answer:

1 option. Enter the desired parameters in the search on the site, select the car you like and go to payment.

Option 2. You need to install the application (iOS or Android), register or enter your personal office and book your desired car. Before you start renting a car, carefully inspect it. The vehicle must be in normal condition without visible damage. If you notice obvious damage, then inform us about it before renting a car.

Question:
Who pays for penalties?
Answer:

Any penalties (for speeding, improper parking, violation of traffic rules) are paid by the client. Renter is notified about fines that needs to be paid. Car owner must noted this in his contract. The renter must send the owner of the car the full amount for a fine.

Question:
Why do you need a blockchain?
Answer:

Blockchain will allow transparency and validation of users, since an unchangeable history of car use, a feedback system, KYC and smart contracts will allow users to trust one another.

Question:
What is special about your token?
Answer:

Token is an important part of the Darenta platform. Using tokens, users will be able to:

• Purchase a deductible for tokens;
• Obtain tokens for the referral program for the given clients who will conclude the transaction;
• Order additional promotion of the proposed car in the platform;
• Rent a car in a closed club, where exclusive cars can be rented only for tokens;
• Receive a discount for the commission of a card-sharing up to 50%;
• Order a professional photo and video review of your car;
• The possibility of getting tokens for creating unique content will be realized by photographers and video bloggers;
• Customers will be able to lease their vehicles for tokens;
• Bloggers will be able to go to press tour in different countries;
• Customers will be able to earn tokens for the posts they make during car rental;
• Payment of tokens for the time spent on the site;
• Payment of tokens for downloading the service application;
• Payment of tokens for filling in the profile;
• It will be possible to obtain information about discounts for nearby cars through a smart clock after integration with IOTA;
• It will be possible to earn tokens, leaving feedback on the car and avtovladeltse after the lease transactions;
• The company will encourage landlords to tokens for feedback on tenants after car rental deals;
• Customers will be able to earn tokens for permission to advertise on their profile page if the ad is viewed enough times, and if there is a required number of conversions from this advertisement;
• Customers will be able to exchange earned tokens for generous prizes in the incentive program;
• Advertising and special projects from advertisers for tokens via smart contracts will be implemented;
• Holders of tokens will be able to sell them in the internal exchanger or on the crypto exchange.
